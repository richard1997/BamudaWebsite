Genie Charts

Genie Charts is an iOS charting app built for technical traders who need powerful analysis tools on the go.

Unlike many mobile charting apps, Genie Charts stores all data locally, allowing you to work online or offline with full access. This design enables advanced and highly customised technical analysis similar to desktop software, but optimised for mobile convenience.

Finger gesture navigation makes chart exploration intuitive and efficient, often easier than desktop charting platforms. In summary, Genie Charts delivers desktop‑like power in a mobile‑friendly experience.
