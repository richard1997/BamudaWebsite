About Bamuda

Bamuda Corporation was established in 2012 as a software consulting company specializing in mobile application development, big data, and cloud computing.

Our team of experts delivers innovative solutions across iOS and Android platforms, cloud infrastructure, and advanced analytics. We pride ourselves on competitive pricing, top quality products, and customer‑first service.

We apply deep expertise in native mobile development, social network analysis, consumer behaviour insights, and big data technologies including machine learning, data mining, statistics, and MapReduce. Our backend solutions leverage C++/C and Node.js/Express.js with MongoDB, supported by agile methodologies to ensure quality and timely delivery.

Bamuda is committed to a culture of honesty, accountability, and excellence. We continue to expand our product portfolio, including Genie Charts for technical traders and TuneAndTime, a musician’s toolkit for tuning and timing.
